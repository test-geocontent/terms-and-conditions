Fictional Stock Picking Contest Rules

Review the official rules, eligibility requirements, scoring process, prize structure, and participant responsibilities for the Fictional Stock Picking Contest 2026.

1. Introduction

The Fictional Stock Picking Contest 2026 is a virtual investment competition designed for educational and entertainment purposes. Participants compete by creating a simulated stock portfolio using virtual funds. No real money is invested, and all trading activity is hypothetical.

2. Eligibility

Participation is open to individuals who are at least eighteen (18) years of age at the time of registration. Each participant may register only one account. Duplicate registrations may result in disqualification.

3. Registration

Registration opens on January 5, 2026, and closes on January 25, 2026. All participants must complete their registration before the deadline. Late registrations will not be accepted.

4. Contest Period

The contest begins on February 1, 2026, and concludes on July 31, 2026. Portfolio performance will be measured throughout this period.

5. Starting Portfolio

Each participant will receive a virtual portfolio valued at $100,000. The funds have no cash value and cannot be exchanged for real currency.

6. Portfolio Requirements

Participants must construct a portfolio consisting of exactly ten (10) stocks. The entire virtual balance must be allocated before the contest begins. No individual stock may represent more than twenty percent (20%) of the portfolio or less than five percent (5%).

Fractional shares are permitted.

7. Portfolio Lock

Once the contest officially begins, portfolios become locked. Participants may not buy, sell, replace, or rebalance any holdings during the contest period.

8. Scoring

Portfolio performance will be calculated based on the percentage increase or decrease in total portfolio value from the starting balance.

The participant with the highest overall portfolio return at the end of the contest will be declared the winner.

9. Tie-Breaking Procedure

If two or more participants achieve identical portfolio returns, the following tie-breakers will be applied in order:

  • The portfolio with the lower overall volatility will rank higher.
  • If a tie still exists, the participant with the earlier registration date and time will rank higher.
  • If a tie remains after these criteria, the winner will be selected through a random draw conducted by the contest organizers.

10. Participant Conduct

Participants are expected to compete fairly and honestly. The use of multiple accounts, automated systems, fraudulent activities, or any attempt to manipulate contest results is strictly prohibited.

The organizers reserve the right to investigate suspicious activity and disqualify participants who violate these rules.

11. Results

Official results will be announced within ten (10) days after the contest concludes. All calculations performed by the contest organizers are considered final.

12. Prizes

The first-place participant will receive a fictional grand prize of $5,000.

The second-place participant will receive a fictional prize of $2,500.

The third-place participant will receive a fictional prize of $1,000.

The remaining participants in the top ten will receive fictional certificates of achievement.

13. Organizer Rights

The organizers reserve the right to amend these rules, postpone contest dates, suspend participation, or cancel the contest if unforeseen circumstances affect the integrity of the competition.

Any significant changes will be announced before they become effective.

14. Acceptance of Rules

By registering for the contest, participants acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to abide by all contest rules and decisions made by the organizers.

15. Disclaimer

This contest is entirely fictional and intended solely as an example of how a stock-picking competition could be organized. All names, dates, prizes, companies, and financial figures are invented for demonstration purposes only and do not represent any actual event or investment opportunity.
