3. Portfolio Selection Rules

3.1. For each Contest entered, participants must select exactly 7 stocks divided into two categories:

  • Starting Line-Up "Starter" Picks: exactly 5 stocks from the designated eligible companies list.
  • Bench Picks: exactly 2 stocks from the designated eligible companies list.

3.2. Participants pick eligible stocks from these conferences: Planet MicroCap June 2026, SmallCap Discoveries September 2026, and LD Micro October 2026.

3.3. The three Contests are independent. A participant who enters all three submits up to 21 stocks total, 15 Starters + 6 Bench.

4. Scoring & Winner Determination

4.1. Performance is ranked by Contest Score, defined as the average peak return of each participant's 5 Starter stocks during that Contest's tracking period. Bench stocks are excluded from scoring except for tiebreakers. Each participant's entry price is locked at the time of their submission, see Competition Rules Section 4 for details.

4.2. Each Contest is scored independently. There is no combined cross-Contest score, and there is no end-of-year final round.

4.3. In the event of a tie within a Contest, two or more participants with identical Contest Scores:

  • Best Bench Performance – The participant whose Bench portfolio peak performance is higher wins.*
  • Best Individual Stock Performance Across Starters and Bench – The participant whose best-performing stock had the highest peak return wins.*
  • Second-Best Individual Stock Performance Across Starters and Bench – If still tied, the second-best performing stock is used.*
  • Earliest Submission – If still tied, the participant who submitted their picks first wins.

*Based on peak return.

4.4. Each participant is allowed only one registered account. The use of multiple email addresses, aliases, or accounts to submit additional portfolios is strictly prohibited.

4.5. If a participant is found to have submitted through multiple accounts, all of their submissions may be disqualified. The organizers reserve the right to verify participant identity and remove duplicate entries.
